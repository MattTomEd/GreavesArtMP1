Catrin Greaves is a Cardiff-based nature photographer.

My photography journey began as a hobby, and has grown into a passion.

I work with a mobile phone camera while starting with my photography journey, often heading out during lockdown periods to take pictures of unique scenes within nature. I also find inspiration from historical art.

I aim to take pictures that showcase the beauty and importance of Wales' natural landscape, and I am able to provide prints that you can use to send as postcards, or to hang up in your room of choice.
