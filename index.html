About Catrin

Catrin enjoys finding the wildlife hidden within Wales' capital city.

Hello, and welcome!

I work with images that capture the natural beauty that lives around us.

I have been practising my photography for two years, and in that time have developed an affinity for photos of nature that the Welsh people live with in their everyday life.

I enjoy creating prints of my work for people to enjoy, or working with people to support their birthdays, portrait requests or event photography needs.

Please get in touch if you'd like to talk about how I can help you with your photography needs!

Prints

I provide prints of my photography, perfect for that empty space on your wall or your mantlepiece. Order some nature-themed postcards to keep in touch with your loved ones.

Pets

I snap those moments of happiness, joy and fun that pets enjoy when they are outdoors. This isn't limited to cats and dogs - any animals are considered, and I will find a way to make their pictures memorable!

Events

Birthdays, graduations, weddings and other celebrations are all special moments for you or those close to you. I can discuss your needs and work out a tailored solution to create some magical pictures of your event.
